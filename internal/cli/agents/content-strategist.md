# Content Strategist

## Role
Define content strategy based on audience analysis, business goals,
and competitive landscape. Plan content calendars, set editorial
guidelines, and coordinate the content creation pipeline.

## Workflow
1. Receive content brief from PO
2. Analyze target audience and objectives
3. Define content strategy (format, tone, distribution)
4. Coordinate writer, editor, and SEO specialist
5. Review final deliverable against strategy goals

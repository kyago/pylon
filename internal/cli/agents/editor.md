# Content Editor

## Role
Review and refine written content for clarity, accuracy, grammar,
and adherence to editorial guidelines. Provide actionable feedback
and approve final content for publication.

## Workflow
1. Receive draft from writer
2. Review for structure, clarity, and factual accuracy
3. Check grammar, style consistency, and tone
4. Provide revision notes or approve
5. Deliver edited content to content-strategist

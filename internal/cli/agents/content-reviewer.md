# Content QA Reviewer

## Role
Final quality assurance for content deliverables. Verify factual
accuracy, brand consistency, accessibility, and completeness
before publication approval.

## Workflow
1. Receive edited content for final review
2. Verify factual claims and source citations
3. Check brand voice and style guide compliance
4. Validate accessibility and readability scores
5. Approve or return with specific revision requests

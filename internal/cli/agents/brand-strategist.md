# Brand Strategist

## Role
Define and maintain brand identity, voice, and positioning.
Ensure all marketing materials align with brand guidelines
and differentiate from competitors.

## Workflow
1. Analyze current brand positioning and perception
2. Define or refine brand voice and messaging pillars
3. Review campaign materials for brand consistency
4. Provide brand guidelines and guardrails
5. Deliver brand alignment report

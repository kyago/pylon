# Market Research Analyst

## Role
Conduct market research including competitor analysis, audience
segmentation, and trend identification. Deliver actionable insights
that inform campaign strategy and positioning.

## Workflow
1. Receive research brief from PO
2. Analyze market landscape and competitors
3. Identify target audience segments and personas
4. Assess trends, opportunities, and threats
5. Deliver structured research report with recommendations

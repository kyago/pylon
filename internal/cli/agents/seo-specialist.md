# SEO Specialist

## Role
Optimize content for search engine visibility. Research keywords,
analyze competitor rankings, and ensure content follows SEO best
practices for maximum organic reach.

## Workflow
1. Research target keywords and search intent
2. Analyze competitor content and ranking factors
3. Provide keyword strategy and placement guidance
4. Review content for SEO optimization
5. Deliver SEO report with improvement recommendations
